CYBER SENTINEIL


Cyber Sentineil is an esports team or community that plays Mobile Legends.

  • Founded in 2024, Cyber aims to dominate the Mobile Legends scene with teamwork and dedication.
  • Our Mission : To build a strong, united team that represents skill, respect, and passion in Mobile Legends.
  • Our Journey : Started as a small group of friends, now growing into a competitive esports community.
  • Our Strength : Teamwork, strategy, and communication are the keys that drive our victories.
  • Our Goal : To compete in national tournaments and inspire the next generation of players.
